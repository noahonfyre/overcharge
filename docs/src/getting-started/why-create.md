# Why Create?
This mod heavily relies on the [Create Mod](https://modrinth.com/mod/create) especially for early game recipes.

## But *why*?
As I am currently the only developer actively working on this project, I just didn't manage to implement my ideas for machines and recipes, which is why I looked for temporary substitutions in Create.

Therefore, they will be replaced in upcoming updates with their custom counterpart, although some concepts will still be similar to those in Create.

## But why *Create*?
I chose Create as it is one of my first and favorite mods, but also as it fits my general idea of the recipes I planned for the future the most.
